Favorite Projects

The following projects are what I would consider my favorite projects, and representative of my abilities and interests.

Pseudo3D

Pseudo3D is a 2.5D game engine written with JavaFX. This engine uses sprite scaling and order of drawing to achieve a fake 3D rendering effect for a 3D physics world. Physics in this engine are AABB (Axis Aligned Bounding Box) basic kinematics.

BoneBot

BoneBot is a self-hosted, modular Discord bot that can respond and react to messages, add text-based commands, and generate memes on demand to add more life to a Discord server.
